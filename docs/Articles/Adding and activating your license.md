<div class="sectionbody">
<div class="paragraph">
<p>Use this procedure when you purchase additional licenses or renew an existing subscription for HP Wolf Pro Security (WPS).</p>
</div>

<table>
<tr>
<td class="icon">
<div class="title">Important</div>

<td class="content">

<div class="sect2">
<h3 id="_before_you_begin">Before you begin</h3>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>Ensure you have:</p>
</div>
<div class="ulist">
<ul>
<li>
<p>Received a license key by email.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Access to the HP Wolf Security Portal.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sect2">
<h3 id="_steps">Steps</h3>
<div class="olist arabic">
<ol class="arabic">
<li>
<p>Sign in to the <a href="https://portal.hpwolf.com"><strong>HP Wolf Security Portal</strong></a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From the <strong>Licenses</strong> dashboard, click <strong>Buy or activate license</strong>.</p>
<div class="imageblock">
<div class="content">
<img src="https://files.us.document360.io/ec5d82e2-88dc-4cc5-9e57-f56ea01dd0b4/Images/Documentation/add-license-dashboard.png" alt="Licenses dashboard with Add License option">
</div>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Activate</strong>.</p>
<div class="imageblock">
<div class="content">
<img src="https://files.us.document360.io/ec5d82e2-88dc-4cc5-9e57-f56ea01dd0b4/Images/Documentation/activiate-license.jpg" alt="Activate existing license">
</div>
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter your <strong>license key</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you have more than one account (tenant), select the correct account from the drop-down list.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Next</strong> to continue.</p>
<div class="imageblock">
<div class="content">
<img src="https://files.us.document360.io/ec5d82e2-88dc-4cc5-9e57-f56ea01dd0b4/Images/Documentation/enter-license-key.jpg" alt="Enter license key screen">

</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>Review the license summary.</p>
<div class="olist loweralpha">
<ol class="loweralpha" type="a">
<li>
<p>Select <strong>I agree to the terms and conditions</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Next</strong>.</p>
<div class="imageblock">
<div class="content">
<img src="https://files.us.document360.io/ec5d82e2-88dc-4cc5-9e57-f56ea01dd0b4/Images/Documentation/license-summary.jpg" alt="License summary and confirmation screen">

</ol>

</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Complete Registration</strong> to finish adding the license.</p>

<div class="content">

<div class="sect1">
<h2 id="_renewing_your_license">Renewing your license</h2>
<div class="sectionbody">
<div class="paragraph">
<p>There are two common renewal scenarios.</p>
</div>
<div class="sect2">
<h3 id="_scenario_1_you_already_have_a_new_license_key">Scenario 1: You already have a new license key</h3>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>If you have already received a new license key, follow the steps in <a href="#_adding_and_activating_a_new_license">Adding and activating a new license</a> to activate it. This is a common scenario after you renew your subscription or if you purchase additional HP Wolf Pro Security (WPS) licenses.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sect2">
<h3 id="_scenario_2_you_do_not_have_a_new_license_key">Scenario 2: You do not have a new license key</h3>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>If you do not yet have a license key:</p>
</div>
<div class="olist arabic">
<ol class="arabic">
<li>
<p>Purchase a license from the HP Store.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Wait for the license email to be delivered to your registered email address.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Follow the steps in <a href="#_adding_and_activating_a_new_license">Adding and activating a new license</a> to activate the license.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sect1">
<h2 id="_how_license_renewal_works">How license renewal works</h2>
<div class="sectionbody">
<div class="paragraph">
<p>After the new license is added to your account:</p>
</div>
<div class="ulist">
<ul>
<li>
<p>Devices continue using their currently assigned license until it expires.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A license expiration warning remains visible in the dashboard until the old license expires. This warning can be safely ignored.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>When the old license expires, devices automatically request and apply an available license.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The expiration date updates automatically, and the license expiration warning is removed.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</div>

No manual reassignment or device action is required.
</td>
</tr>

<div class="sect1">
<h2 id="_i_have_not_received_a_license_key">I have not received a license key</h2>
<div class="sectionbody">
<div class="paragraph">
<p>If you purchased HP Wolf Pro Security (WPS) but have not received a license key:</p>
</div>
<div class="ulist">
<ul>
<li>
<p>Check the email address you provided during the purchase or trial registration of HP Wolf Pro Security (WPS).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Allow at least four hours for the email to arrive.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Check your junk or spam folder.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If the license email is still missing, contact the reseller or vendor you purchased from. For purchases made through the HP Store, contact HP Store Order Support.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
